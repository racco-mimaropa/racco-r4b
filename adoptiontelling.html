Adoption Telling

Telling your child that he/she is adopted may be challenging but a child's adoption story is a crucial aspect of one's development, identity formation, and emotional well-being. Adoption telling is an ongoing process that requires sensitivity, empathy, and honesty. By sharing their adoption story, parents can help their child develop a positive sense of identity, foster trust and openness, and promote emotional well-being.
